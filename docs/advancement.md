## Advancement

The blink dog companion advances as its master advances. Its progression is tied to the player character's character level, representing a deepening supernatural bond and growing fey prowess.

### Companion Advancement Levels

Beginning when the player character reaches 4th level, the blink dog gains a **companion advancement levels**.

Each time the blink dog gains a companion advancement level, it gains the benefit listed for the player character’s level in the table below.

* The companion cannot increase an ability score above **20** during advancement.
* Before level 4, the companion uses its base stat block.

Levels without advancement represent the companion’s bond deepening without manifesting new supernatural abilities.
