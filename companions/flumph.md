### Psionic Abilities and Special Traits

Some flumph skills or attacks resemble spells or magical effects.

* These abilities are **innate psionic effects**, not spellcasting.
* The flumph does not gain spell slots.
* No verbal, somatic, or material components are required.
* Any saving throw DC for flumph abilities is calculated as follows:
  **8 + the flumph’s proficiency bonus + the flumph’s Wisdom modifier**

### Action Economy and Control
* The flumph acts on its own initiative.
* It can use actions, bonus actions, and reactions as described in its stat block and learned abilities.
* Any ability that requires the player character’s reaction or coordination explicitly states so.

## Flumph Companion Feats

### Psychic Dampener
Enemies within 10 feet of the flumph have disadvantage on the first attack roll they make each turn.

### Empathic Shield
When an ally within 30 feet takes damage, the flumph can use its reaction to reduce the damage by **1d8 + the flumph’s proficiency bonus**.

This reaction can be used a number of times equal to the flumph’s proficiency bonus per long rest.

### Thought Static
Creatures poisoned by the flumph’s **Stench Spray** have disadvantage on Wisdom saving throws until the end of their next turn.

### Aberrant Insight
The flumph gains expertise in **Insight**.
It also has advantage on Insight checks made to detect hostile intent or emotional manipulation.

### Mind Anchor
While the flumph is within 10 feet of its master, neither can be charmed or frightened.


### Psionic Recoil

When the flumph is required to make a saving roll against a spell or magical effect, the caster takes psychic damage equal to twice the flumph’s proficiency bonus.

## New Attacks

### Psychic Pulse

***Psychic Pulse.*** *Ranged Psionic Attack:* +4 to hit, range 30 ft., one target.
*Hit:* 1d8 psychic damage.
The target has disadvantage on its next attack roll before the end of its next turn.

### Disorienting Tentacle

***Disorienting Tentacle.*** *Melee Weapon Attack:* +4 to hit, reach 5 ft., one target.
*Hit:* 1d6 + 2 bludgeoning damage.
The target’s speed is reduced to 0 until the start of its next turn.

### Mind Ripple

***Mind Ripple.*** *Psionic Burst:* Each creature of the flumph’s choice within 10 feet must succeed on a Wisdom saving throw or take 1d6 psychic damage and have disadvantage on its next saving throw before the end of its next turn.

### Psychic Rebound

***Psychic Rebound.*** *Reaction.*
When a creature within 15 feet hits the flumph or its master with an attack, the attacker takes psychic damage equal to the flumph’s proficiency bonus and has disadvantage on its next attack roll.

## New Skills

### Empathic Scan

As a bonus action, the flumph learns the emotional state and hostile intent of creatures within 30 feet until the start of its next turn.

### Psychic Veil

Once per long rest, the flumph and its master become invisible to creatures relying on telepathy, empathy, or psychic senses for 1 minute.

### Hover Guard

While the flumph is adjacent to its master, the master has advantage on saving throws against charm and fear effects.

### Mental Static

Creatures within 10 feet of the flumph have disadvantage on the first concentration check they make each round.
